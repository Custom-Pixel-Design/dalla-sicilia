Menu:

The finest traditional Sicilian recipes, made with only the freshest and highest quality ingredients. From tantalizing appetizers, to filling and delicious mains, and mouthwateringly crave-worthy desserts, we're bringing the flavors of Sicily right here to Texas!

Appetizers:

Arancini, $10.00, Fried balls of Sicilian risotto stuffed with mozzarella cheese. Served with marinara.

Caprese Salad, $7.00, A fresh salad made from tomato wedges, sliced mozzarella, and fresh basil garnished with olive oil, salt, and capers.

Main Dish:

Muffuletta, $12.00, Sandwich made on Sicilian crispy sesame bread with mortadella, salami, cappacuolo, provolone, tomato, olive salad, pesto, and relish.

Pasta all’arrabbiata, $15.00, Penne pasta served in a spicy tomato sauce consisting of garlic, tomatoes, dried red chili peppers, and olive oil.

Dessert:

Cannoli, $8.00, A tube-shaped Italian dessert that consists of fried pastry dough stuffed with a sweet, creamy cheese filling, garnished with chocolate shavings.

Tiramisu, $11.00, Slice of heaven made from pastries dipped in coffee and layered with whipped egg yolks, sugar, and mascarpone cheese. Sprinkled with cocoa powder. Served with a shot of espresso.
