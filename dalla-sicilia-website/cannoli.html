Cannoli

A tube-shaped Italian dessert that consists of fried pastry dough stuffed with a sweet, creamy cheese filling, garnished with chocolate shavings.


Allergy: Gluten and dairy.


Dietary Info: 215 calories, 11.7 g fat, 22.1 g carbs and 4.4 g protein.
Ingredient List: All-purpose flour, granulated sugar, salt, unsalted butter, egg yolk, dry white wine, ricotta cheese, powdered sugar, ground cinnamon, allspice, heavy cream, semisweet chocolate shavings, lemon, canola oil, and powdered sugar.



Operating Hours: 11:00 AM - 9:30 PM; Tuesday - Saturday Closed; Sunday - Monday
