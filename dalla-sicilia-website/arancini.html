Arancini

Fried balls of Sicilian risotto stuffed with mozzarella cheese. Served with marinara.


Allergy: Gluten and dairy.


Dietary Info: 252 calories per serving, with 16g of fat, 19g of carbs, and 6g of protein.
Ingredient List: Extra virgin olive oil, yellow onion, garlic cloves, rice, salt, finely ground black pepper, Italian parsley leaves, homemade chicken stock, double-concentrated tomato paste, unsalted butter, grated parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, all-purpose flour, egg, panko breadcrumbs, and vegetable oil.



Operating Hours: 11:00 AM - 9:30 PM; Tuesday - Saturday Closed; Sunday - Monday

Address: 321 W Main St, Italy, TX 76651
